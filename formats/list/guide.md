## Observed content technique

The captured format appears to use:

- a hook that creates tension through a counterintuitive or disappointing outcome;
- a recognizable list of concrete behaviors rather than an abstract explanation;
- first-person or close-to-reader language that makes the situation feel privately familiar;
- a progression from visible behavior toward a reframe or realization.

These are observations from the captured artifact, not validated env performance rules.
